The First Wave

2019 | Oil on Canvas | 50 x 40 cm

In this image Neptune is mad at the person responsible for dropping the plastic bottle in the ocean, and Jupiter agrees. Humanity leaves traces of their appearance in this world for many years to come, and future generations will have to deal with our laziness. They will have to live on a budget trying to repair all of our mistakes.
