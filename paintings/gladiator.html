Gladiator

2019 | Oil on Canvas-Board | 24 x 30 cm

Caught stealing bread to feed his family, since he didn't have the resources to buy it, he awaits judgment by fighting for the masses' entertainment. Now the bars are both safety and imprisonment. His situation is hopeless but all he can think about is the hunger of his family while the crowd goes wild.
