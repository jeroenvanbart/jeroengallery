Caught by the illuminated side of the livingroom

2021 | Oil on linen canvas | 60 x 80 cm

Nowadays we are so used to looking at screens that even the dimensions of a television will steer our eyes in it's direction. Screens capture our attention and this is valuable, for whole companies are built with our attention as their businessmodel. But our lack of attention can be even more precious since we can only devote our focus to one concept. Despite their poverty or wealth everybody has acces to the wonders of the world arround their home or beyond. Art, music, sunsets, nature and different forms of entertainment are always close by. Did you decide what to devote your focus on?
