Caught by the illuminated side of the livingroom

2021, Oil on linen canvas, 60*80 CM

Nowadays we are so used to looking at screens that even the dimensions of a television will steer your eye. Screens capture our attention and our attention is very valuable, for whole companies are built with our attention as their businessmodel. But our lack of attention for the right things is bad for the planet, since we can only be at one place at the same time. Poverty, joker, hoping for luck and dreaming of better days while doing nothing but picking eaten apples, or soak up nature, art and all the other wonders of the world. But music ain't all that bad.
