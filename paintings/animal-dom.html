Animal Domination in a Borderless World

2020 | Oil on Linen Canvas | 80 x 120 cm

When you look at clouds long enough it's inevitable that some shapes start making sense. The human brain is hardwired to regonize familiar things just like the brain takes some rules for granted. Gravity and human superiority are two of those rules. In this painting the rules of gravity don't apply neither does human superiority, even though there are traces of humanity the animals are in control.
