Alien Anatomy Lesson

2018 | Oil on Canvas | 30 x 40 cm

Reflecting over how far humanity came in the past 400 years since Rembrandt van Rijn made his masterpiece The Anatomy Lesson of Dr. Nicolaes Tulp, I couldn't help but wonder what will happen in the next 400 years. At the rate we are developing new technologies and exploring unknown parts of the universe there is a chance that we will encounter a new form of life someday. Then it will be time to go back to the dissecting table.
