Alien Anatomy Lesson

2018 | Oil on Canvas | 30 x 40 cm

Reflecting over how far humanity came in the past 400 years since Rembrandt van Rijn made his masterpiece The Anatomy Lesson of Dr. Nicolaes Tulp, I couldn't help myself wondering what would happen in the next 400 years. At the rate we are developing new technologies, got an understanding about the human body and exploring unknown parts of the universe there is a chance that we will encounter a form of life someday. Capturing this pondering in one image is the Alien Anatomy Lesson.
