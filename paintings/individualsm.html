Individualism

2020 | Oil on linen canvas | 80 x 60 cm

The worlds invitation to play the game of individualism. Children have an honest way of looking at life, they wear their hearts out in the open and hold magical expectations about the future. Adults are more cautious living their live highly influenced by society, almost like life is a one way track with little control over the way they are heading. Most directions come fortuitously or after hard work and dedication while society makes pretty big decisions for us. The decisions we do control have to be taken wisely for we can not turn back time. The backworths clock is broken.
