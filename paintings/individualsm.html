Individualism

2020 | Oil on Linen Canvas | 80 x 60 cm

Children have an honest way of looking at life. They wear their hearts out in the open and hold the most magical expectations about the future. Adults, however, are much more cautious. Living their lives influenced by society, adults often act almost as if life were a one-way forward track with little to no control over its course. Direction comes fortuitously or after much hard work and dedication while society makes the big decisions for us. Nevertheless, the decisions we do control must be taken wisely for the course of time cannot be altered. The backwards clock is broken.

The world's invitation to play the game of individualism.
