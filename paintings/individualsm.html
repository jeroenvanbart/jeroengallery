Individualism

2020, Oil on linen canvas, 80*60 CM

Young untroubled minds with magical expectations about the future are proof that ignorance is bliss. Skies are clear and they fearlessly show their heart to anyone that cares, in the hope someone influences it in a positive matter. It is when we grow older we realize that our choices are only part of the directions we take in life. There is a certain factor of chance in which cards you are dealt, the family you grow up in and the recources availible for your development into adulthood. Also decisions others make can have a great impact on life. But the choices we can make should be he right ones at the time, for we can not turn back time.
